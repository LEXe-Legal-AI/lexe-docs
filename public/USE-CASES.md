# LEXe Legal AI - Use Cases

## Studio Legale Piccolo (1-5 Avvocati)

### The Challenge

Small firms handle diverse cases with limited resources. Associates spend hours on normative research that could be invested in client strategy and courtroom preparation.

### How LEXe Helps

- **Ricerca normativa rapida**: Ask a question in natural Italian and receive a structured answer with citations to Normattiva, Cassazione maxims, and relevant doctrine -- in minutes instead of hours.
- **Pareri preliminari**: Generate a first-draft legal opinion with source references that the attorney reviews and refines, cutting drafting time significantly.
- **Aggiornamento normativo**: Verify whether a norm has been amended, repealed, or supplemented by subsequent legislation using the vigenza checker.
- **Massimario access**: Search over 38,000 curated maxims from the Court of Cassation without navigating multiple databases manually.

### Example Scenario

> An attorney needs to advise a client on prescription terms for a traffic accident damages claim. LEXe retrieves Art. 2947 c.c., relevant Cassazione interpretations on the distinction between contractual and extracontractual liability, and recent amendments -- all cited and confidence-rated.

## Studio Legale Medio (5-20 Avvocati)

### The Challenge

Mid-size firms manage multiple practice areas simultaneously. Consistency across attorneys, knowledge sharing, and efficient case preparation become critical at this scale.

### How LEXe Helps

- **Compliance check**: Verify client operations against current regulatory frameworks. LEXe cross-references norms, implementing regulations, and relevant jurisprudence.
- **Contenzioso preparation**: Research opposing arguments, find supporting and adverse precedents, and identify gaps in the evidentiary record before trial.
- **Recovery crediti**: Analyze applicable procedures (decreto ingiuntivo, esecuzione forzata) with current thresholds, fees, and procedural requirements.
- **Knowledge retention**: The memory system preserves research context across conversations, so work done on a case six months ago is still accessible.
- **Team consistency**: Shared personas and tenant-wide knowledge ensure that all attorneys in the firm receive consistent, high-quality research.

### Example Scenario

> A firm handling a commercial lease dispute needs to verify the current state of Art. 1571-1614 c.c., relevant Cassazione positions on tacit renewal, and applicable procedural deadlines. LEXe's multi-agent research retrieves norms, jurisprudence, and doctrine in parallel, flagging where sources disagree.

## Corporate Legal Department

### The Challenge

In-house legal teams face high volumes of routine inquiries alongside complex regulatory compliance obligations. Speed and accuracy must coexist.

### How LEXe Helps

- **Risk assessment**: Evaluate contractual clauses against current law and recent jurisprudence. Identify clauses that may be void, voidable, or unenforceable.
- **NDA and contract triage**: Quickly identify non-standard clauses, missing protections, and regulatory conflicts in incoming agreements.
- **Compliance audit**: Check internal policies and procedures against applicable regulations (privacy, labor, corporate governance) with citation-backed findings.
- **Regulatory monitoring**: Track legislative developments relevant to the company's sector with structured summaries and impact assessment.

### Example Scenario

> A corporate legal team receives 15 NDAs per week from potential partners. LEXe assists by highlighting clauses that deviate from the company's standard terms, flagging jurisdiction-specific risks, and verifying that non-compete provisions comply with current antitrust norms.

## Legal Tech Partner

### The Challenge

Legal technology companies need reliable legal AI capabilities embedded in their own products, without building and maintaining the entire legal knowledge infrastructure.

### How LEXe Helps

- **API integration**: A documented REST API with SSE streaming allows embedding LEXe's legal research capabilities into any application.
- **White-label potential**: Tenant-level configuration of personas, models, and tools enables customized experiences under the partner's brand.
- **Multi-tenant architecture**: Each partner's clients operate in isolated tenants with independent data, configuration, and billing.
- **Scalable plans**: Plan-based resource allocation (users, conversations, tokens) supports flexible commercial models from freemium to enterprise.

### Example Scenario

> A legal document management platform integrates LEXe's chat API to offer in-context legal research. When a user opens a contract, they can ask LEXe questions about applicable norms, and the response appears within the document management interface -- complete with citations and confidence ratings.
